Home

With a love and passion for contemporary art, Nate Wilde started Najawi Gallery to share with others. It aims at becoming a world renowned gallery and bringing viewers and collectors to Salt Lake City, Utah, where it is located. Some say it is Utah's finest contemporary art gallery.

More than a gallery: Najawi captivates and connects with audiences. It is about the community. This gallery features artists from around the world, giving them a voice. Najawi has shown work in several past exhibitions and has many to come. The gallery started with its 2008 debut exhibtion, "Two Lines Align" with artist Geoff Mcfetridge.
